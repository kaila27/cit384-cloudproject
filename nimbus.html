Different Kinds of Clouds

☁ Nimbus Clouds ☁

These mid-level clouds are often accompanied by continuous moderate rain or snow and appear to cover most of the sky. Nimbostratus will often bring precipitation which may last for several hours. When temperatures are cold enough, these clouds may also contain ice particles and snow. The formation of clouds is also affected by the movement of air currents. These clouds are formed because of warm weather conditions.
